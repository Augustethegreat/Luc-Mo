Contact Us

Contact Us

01. What types of wines does LucMo Wines offer?

LucMo Wines offers a carefully curated selection of premium South African wines, including a variety of reds, whites, and sparkling wines. We work in partnership with renowned wineries across the Cape Winelands, selecting each wine to highlight the unique flavors and high quality that South Africa is known for. Whether you’re looking for a full-bodied red, a crisp white, or an elegant sparkling option, our wines capture the essence of South Africa’s rich wine heritage, providing options for every taste and occasion.

02. Can individuals purchase directly from LucMo Wines, or is it only for businesses?

Yes, individuals are more than welcome to purchase directly from LucMo Wines! While we proudly supply high-quality wines to restaurants, hotels, guesthouses, and corporate clients, we also offer a seamless experience for individual buyers. Whether you're buying for personal enjoyment, adding to your collection, or selecting a special gift, we make it easy for everyone to access our premium wines. Our goal is to provide both personal and business clients with exceptional service and a selection that meets every need.

03. Does LucMo Wines host wine tastings?

Absolutely! Our tasting room provides an immersive experience where guests can enjoy a guided tour through our diverse range of wines. Perfect for individuals, groups, or corporate events, our tastings offer insights into each wine’s story, origins, and ideal pairings. Surrounded by the beauty of our winery setting, you’ll have the chance to appreciate the craftsmanship behind each bottle, making it an ideal outing for wine enthusiasts and newcomers alike who want to experience the best of South African wine.

04. Do you offer delivery services for orders?

Yes, we offer convenient delivery services for orders within South Africa and international shipping to selected destinations. We take extra care in packaging to ensure that each bottle arrives in perfect condition, whether it's for a local event, a personal order, or a corporate gift. Our team is also available to assist with special requests or shipping requirements, making it easy to enjoy LucMo Wines no matter where you are.

05. What events are LucMo Wines suitable for?

LucMo Wines are the perfect addition to a wide variety of events, from intimate gatherings to large celebrations. Our wines are ideal for sharing during leisure moments with friends, family gatherings, weddings, and corporate events, adding a touch of elegance and quality. They’re also excellent for wine tastings, gifting, or any occasion that calls for something memorable. With our selection, you can create magical moments, elevating any event with the unique and premium flavors of South African wine

06. Does LucMo Wines offer wine pairing recommendations?

Absolutely! At LucMo Wines, we’re passionate about helping you enjoy our wines to their fullest potential. Our knowledgeable team can provide personalized pairing suggestions for any of our wines, whether you’re planning a meal, hosting an event, or simply curious about the best flavors to complement your selection. From pairing bold reds with hearty dishes to finding the perfect white for lighter fare, we’re here to help make your wine experience exceptional. Just reach out to us, and we’ll guide you in choosing the ideal pairings for any occasion.
